# WhOregonTrail
Whoregon Trail Game
I want to take the original concept for the original Oregon trail and make a modern interpretation of it, while capturing a Mel Brooks inspired sense of humor, and true to Brooks, through the comedy can be pretty serious points that you can make.   
Game Synopsis:
A group of 9 characters (8 + Player) must make it to Oregon to start a better life.   Oregon in this game will represent Heaven, Zion, Gaia, etc, the ultimate symbol of hope at the time.  However, there will be a stable of at least 20 characters with wildly varying characteristics (see: Characteristics).  The interactions, frictions, hookups, etc between the characters will all be AI procedurally generated during the game.  I want it to as closely resemble real life as possible, so I want religion in here, I want trans and LGBTQA people in there, all races, etc.   Activities on the trail must also depict interactions with native americans in a respectful and historically accurate way as well.  Even if that shows a negative impact on their lives after we have been through their land.  Here’s the twist though.  This is a traveling burlesque show, and the actors are also sex workers.  That’s how we are going to make the money to get to Oregon.  Since we have AI now, I also want to bring back the type-box style of game play for conversations, except players would ideally speak into their headset, then AI would take it from there.   I would like each trip to take around 6-10 hours.  I know the original was quick, but the point of this is to drink in the interactions between the diverse cast.   

Gameplay Outline:
1.	Starting:  You must pick 4 remaining characters, the computer will select the others. 
a.	Wagon will be inspired right from Oregon trail, we will need food, water, wagon parts, oxen, horses, weapons for hunting, etc.  
2.	Once your team is selected, there will be a bar meeting for that night’s show, as the 4 members the computer has selected are existing members.  The AI will generate interaction between everyone and the player.  The player will have to type responses, that will be interpreted by the AI for how it is taken by each of the 8 players.   Player will have a mini game for that nights show, which earns money for the initial purchases.  I feel like a mix of spider man and arkham knight style mechanics for some kind of dance number would be fun.  
3.	On the road would be a red dead redemption style traveling game for the player.   Meanwhile, the AI is continuing to progress relationships between the players, letting the player overhear the most important dialogue (changing relationship statuses the most).  The player will also be able to choose speed, monitor health, avoid dangers, pick up side missions, and most importantly, stop for hunting.  As you know, this was the part everyone loved the most in the original Oregon trail.  I want a modernized version of the original hunting mini game.  
4.	Forts:  The forts will serve as mission stops for our crew.   This will represent the opportunity for the player to control all 9 people for their side missions.   This is where I want it to suddenly take on the feel of the hitman games.  There’s 9 people whom can all choose how much they are willing to sacrifice for the team.  I want missions with multiple options to solve.  I want missions where perhaps sexual favors could be traded for say money or treasure maps or keys to get someone out of jail, etc.  However, that person has developed bonds with the crew, and maybe that decision to complete that mission will then jeopardize the other relationships.  
5.	On the road again:  between forts is where all of the players decisions as the crew will show up and now take on a mass effect style feel where the AI drives the relationships in whatever direction is forced by the decisions the player made.  There will be no side stepping a crew member who feels so mad he leaves at the next stop or even deserts in the middle of the night, and you have to replace them at the next fort (and now we have to consider what that will do to the moral of the crew)
6.	We need a finale for the last stop.  I remember the rafting mini game at the end.  It should be an orgy.  There has to be tough missions for each player to complete at the orgy.
